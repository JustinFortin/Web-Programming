Hi, my name is Justin-Thomas Warkus-Fortin, and this a short biography. I was born and raised right here in Ottawa, Ontario. I was the first born and then 3 years later my brother was born. As my brother and I got older, my parents got married with my brother and I being a part of it. I grew up going to École élémentaire catholique Terre-des-Jeunes, an all french catholic school for all of my primary years. In seventh grade I decided to follow my science passion and enroll at a science oriented school program at College Catholique Samuel-Genest. Unfortunately long commutes were not fun, it got worse everyday. Therefore the year after, my parents agreed and changed me to College Catholique Franco-Ouest to finish my high school diploma.

Up until college applications, my target career was to be an electrician, but due to my asthma and back problems, I decided to follow my second choice. Never did I think that this would have been the best decision so far in my life. I have finally found a something that makes me want to get up and learn everyday.

As I advance in my schooling, the excitment to get out on the job market and to get started on my career has truly made everything from working full-time, part-time, struggling at school, worth it for me because of the passion I have developped for my program of study.
