Our mission at ALOW is to help give back to anyone and everyone that we possibly can! We support a wide variety of different causes such as:

  • Helping people to defeat poverty through education and
  • Empowering women especially widows
  • Building home for homeless
  • Providing pure drinking water
  • Helping to obtain hygienic practice in daily life
  • Guiding how to deal with difficult situations in life
  • Providing emergency services
  • Distributing relief goods in case of natural calamity
