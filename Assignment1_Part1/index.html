ALOW is a non-profit organization based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. We are an organization dedicated to helping a diverse group of people locally with the hopes of expanding on a global scale over the next few years. Our goal is to provide assistance to less fortunate, orphans, and widows through several different options including donating, volunteering as well as campaigning and fundraising.
